1. Make the dough
2. Shape the dough into a thin circle 2' across and 1/4" deep
3. Roll the edges so they are 3/4" thick
4. Generously, spread 2 cups of Beau Jo's marinara sauce up to the rolled edges
5. Evenly spread 3# of Beau Jo's cheese mix
6. Bake the pizza until done
7. Select the box for a 3# pie
8. Box the piping hot pizza with 3 packets of honey for the rolled crust
9. Ask delivery person to deliver the pizza to the specified address
